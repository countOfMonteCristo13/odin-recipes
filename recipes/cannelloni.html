Cannelloni Recipe

Description

Fresh pasta stuffed with a rich ragù, topped with creamy béchamel and plenty of Parmesan and then baked until golden and bubbling.

Ingredients

  • 1kg lean minced beef
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large oninon, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 x 660g jars passata with basil
  • large pinch caster sugar
  • 400g driend cannelloni tubes

For the topping

  • 50g butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 600ml whole milk
  • 140g soft cheese with garlic and herbs
  • 140g parmesan,grated

Steps

  1. Dry-fry the beef in a non-stick pan on a medium-high heat - 10min
  2. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add the oil and cook the onion for 5 mins, until soft
  4. Add the garlic for 1 min more, then tip in the beef and 1½ jars of the passata sauce and the sugar
  5. Simmer for 20 mins while you make the white sauce
  6. Heat the butter in a small pan
  7. When foaming, stir in the flour for 1 min.
  8. Add the milk gradually, stirring all the time to achieve a lump-free sauce
  9. Remove from the heat and stir in the soft cheese with seasoning until dissolved
  10. Set aside, covered with cling film to stop a skin forming
  11. Pour the remaining tomato sauce into the base of 2-3 large baking dishes
  12. Spoon the beef into the cannelloni tubes using a teaspoon and lay on top of the sauce
  13. Pour on the white sauce, then sprinkle with Parmesan
  14. To eat straight away, heat oven to 200C/180C fan/gas 6
  15. Cook for 40-45 mins until the pasta is tender and topping golden.
