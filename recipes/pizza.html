Pizza Recipe

Description

Italin dish consisting of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with some combination of olive oil, oregano, tomato, olives, mozzarella or other cheese, and many other ingredients, baked quickly—usually, in a commercial setting, using a wood-fired oven heated to a very high temperature—and served hot.

Ingredients

For the base

  • 300g strong bread flour
  • 1 tbsp instant yiest
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oli, plus extra for drizzling

For the tomato sauce

  • 100ml passata
  • handful fresh basil of 1 tsp dried
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed

For the topping

  • 125g ball mozzarella, sliced
  • handful grated or shaved parmesan (or vegetarian alternative)
  • handful of cherry tomatoes, halved

Steps

Base

  1. Put the flour into a large bowl, then stir in the yeast and salt.
  2. Pour in 200ml warm water and the olive oil and bring together with a wooden spoon until you have a soft, fairly wet dough
  3. Turn onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 5 mins until smooth
  4. Cover with a tea towel and set aside
  5. You can leave the dough to rise if you like, but it’s not essential for a thin crust

Sauce

  1. Mix the passata, basil and crushed garlic together, then season to taste
  2. Leave to stand at room temperature while you get on with shaping the base

Dough

  1. Roll out the dough
  2. If you’ve let the dough rise, give it a quick knead, then split into two balls
  3. On a floured surface, roll out the dough into large rounds, about 25cm across, using a rolling pin
  4. The dough needs to be very thin as it will rise in the oven
  5. Lift the rounds onto two floured baking sheets

Bake

  1. Heat the oven to 240C/220C fan/gas 8.
  2. Put another baking sheet or an upturned baking tray in the oven on the top shelf
  3. Smooth sauce over bases with the back of a spoon
  4. Scatter with cheese and tomatoes, drizzle with olive oil and season
  5. Put one pizza, still on its baking sheet, on top of the preheated sheet or tray
  6. Bake for 8-10 mins until crisp
  7. Serve with a little more olive oil, and basil leaves if using
  8. Repeat step for remaining pizza
